Internal Policies

Guidelines that shape our workplace

Code of Conduct

Our commitment to ethics, respect, and integrity. All employees must adhere to the highest standards of professional behavior, including anti-corruption, confidentiality, and respect for diversity.

Leave Policy

Annual leave: 21 working days per year. Sick leave: 30 days per cycle (fully paid). Family responsibility leave: 5 days per year. Maternity/Paternity leave as per Lesotho Labour Code.

Data Privacy & IT Usage

Protecting employee and company data is paramount. Use of company devices must comply with IT security guidelines. Personal data is processed in accordance with the Data Protection Act.

Training & Development Policy

Employees are entitled to 5 training days per year. Sponsorship for accredited programs is subject to approval and retention agreement.

For full policy documents, contact HR at hr@lesothohr.co.ls
